American Airlines Customer Service Guide

To contact a live representative at American Airlines, call their 24/7 customer service hotline at +1-(866)-262-9736 or 1-800-American Airlines. You can also use their website’s live chat or email for assistance. Whether you're changing a flight, handling a booking issue, or need general support, speaking with a live agent is the fastest way to get help.
